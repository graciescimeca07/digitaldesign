Digital Designer Career

By Gracie Scimeca

Detailed Description of Career

A Motion Graphics Designer creates animated visuals that bring graphics to life. They work with video, special effects, 3D models, and typography to produce engaging content for various platforms, like social media, commercials, apps, and movies.

Roles and Duties

  • Design and animate graphics for video content
  • Collaborate with teams to ensure animations align with brand style and vision
  • Work with audio to synchronize animation with sound
  • Create storyboards and visual concepts before production

Salary

Entry Level: $50,000 - $60,000 per year
Experienced: $70,000 - $100,000 per year, depending on location and company
